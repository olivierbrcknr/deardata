Liebe Daten,*

* german for "Dear Data"

In 2018 Niko lived in San Francisco, Olivier in Munich. We called each other regularly and chatted a lot, but we wanted to experiment with an other form of communication as well.

We were both fascinated by the book "Dear Data" by Giorgia Lupi and Stefanie Posavec and wanted to take the opportunity of the distance between us to also collect, draw, and send data to each other.

On this website, you can find the postcards that resulted from this project. We did this for six weeks. We hope you enjoy it!
